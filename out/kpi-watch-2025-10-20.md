## 1. EFRAG FRB & EFRAG SRB Joint Hybrid Meeting
**Summary:**  
On October 14, 2025, the European Financial Reporting Advisory Group (EFRAG) will host a joint hybrid meeting between the Financial Reporting Board (FRB) and the Sustainability Reporting Board (SRB). This meeting is designed for registered observers and will take place from 13:00 to 15:00 CET. The agenda and supporting documents will be accessible online, and participants can register to watch the live webcast or access an on-demand recording. The meeting may include closed sessions, ensuring focused discussions on critical topics in financial and sustainability reporting.

*What changes for reporters:*
- Increased transparency in sustainability reporting practices discussed during the meeting.
- Potential updates to reporting standards that may affect future disclosures.

*Data & controls:*
- Access to meeting agendas and documents will be crucial for accurate reporting.
- Observers must register to ensure compliance with participation protocols.

**Tags:** EFRAG, Sustainability Reporting, Financial Reporting  
**Sources:** [EFRAG News](https://www.efrag.org/en/news-and-calendar/news/efrag-frb-efrag-srb-joint-hybrid-meeting-14-october-2025)

## 2. EFRAG FRB & EFRAG FR TEG Joint Webcast Meeting
**Summary:**  
Also on October 14, 2025, EFRAG will conduct a joint webcast meeting between the Financial Reporting Board (FRB) and the Financial Reporting Technical Expert Group (FR TEG) from 09:00 to 10:30 CET. This meeting is open to duly registered observers and aims to discuss key developments in financial reporting standards. Participants can register to view the live webcast or access the recording later. As with other EFRAG meetings, closed sessions may occur, emphasizing the importance of confidentiality in certain discussions.

*What changes for reporters:*
- New insights into financial reporting standards that may influence future reporting practices.
- Opportunities to engage with experts on emerging financial reporting issues.

*Data & controls:*
- Observers will need to ensure they have the latest documents and agenda for accurate reporting.
- Registration is mandatory to access the meeting content.

**Tags:** EFRAG, Financial Reporting, Webcast  
**Sources:** [EFRAG News](https://www.efrag.org/en/news-and-calendar/news/efrag-frb-efrag-fr-teg-joint-webcast-meeting-14-october-2025)

## 3. EFRAG SRB & SR TEG Joint Webcast Meeting
**Summary:**  
On the same day, October 14, 2025, EFRAG will also host a joint webcast meeting between the Sustainability Reporting Board (SRB) and the Sustainability Reporting Technical Expert Group (SR TEG) from 09:00 to 10:40 CET. This meeting is open to registered observers and will cover significant topics related to sustainability reporting standards. Participants can register to attend the live session or view the recording afterward. As with previous meetings, there may be closed sessions to facilitate in-depth discussions.

*What changes for reporters:*
- Enhanced understanding of sustainability reporting standards that may impact future disclosures.
- Engagement with technical experts on sustainability reporting challenges.

*Data & controls:*
- Access to meeting materials is essential for accurate reporting and compliance.
- Registration is required to participate and access meeting content.

**Tags:** EFRAG, Sustainability Reporting, Webcast  
**Sources:** [EFRAG News](https://www.efrag.org/en/news-and-calendar/news/efrag-srb-sr-teg-joint-webcast-meeting-14-october-2025)
