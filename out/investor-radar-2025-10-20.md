## 1. How to Decarbonise Global Transport and Industry?
- **Deal Summary**: The International Renewable Energy Agency (IRENA) has published a report outlining strategies for decarbonizing global transport and industry. The report emphasizes the need for innovative technologies, policy frameworks, and financial investments to achieve significant reductions in carbon emissions across these sectors.
  
- **Why it matters**: Transport and industry are major contributors to global greenhouse gas emissions, accounting for approximately 24% and 21% of total emissions, respectively. Effective decarbonization strategies are crucial for meeting international climate targets and transitioning to a sustainable economy.

- **Risks/unknowns**: The implementation of proposed strategies may face challenges such as regulatory hurdles, technological feasibility, and the need for substantial financial investments. Additionally, the effectiveness of these strategies will depend on global cooperation and commitment from various stakeholders.
