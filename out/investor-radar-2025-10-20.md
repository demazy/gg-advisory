## 1. Closing the Financing Gap in the Global South Requires a Multi-Pronged Approach
- **Deal Summary**: The International Renewable Energy Agency (IRENA) highlights the need for a cumulative investment of USD 31.5 trillion by 2030 to meet renewable energy capacity targets. Currently, only 10% of global investments in renewable energy are reaching developing economies, which face higher risks and uncertainties.
- **Why it matters**: Addressing the financing gap is crucial for achieving global renewable energy targets and ensuring equitable energy access. Increased investment in the Global South can stimulate economic growth and reduce poverty.
- **Risks/unknowns**: High perceived risks in emerging markets, such as currency volatility and policy uncertainty, may deter investors. The effectiveness of international cooperation in increasing financial flows remains uncertain.
- **Tags**: #Investment #RenewableEnergy #GlobalSouth #IRENA
- **Sources**: [IRENA Article](https://www.irena.org/News/articles/2025/May/Closing-the-Financing-Gap-in-the-Global-South-Requires-a-Multi-Pronged-Approach)

## 2. AFID Enterprise Twinning Platform Connecting Businesses for Industrial Decarbonization
- **Deal Summary**: IRENA has initiated a pilot twinning program to connect small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in the renewable energy sector with larger companies. This program aims to enhance industrial decarbonization efforts through peer mentorship and collaboration.
- **Why it matters**: Strengthening the role of SMEs in the energy transition is vital for innovation and market development. The twinning program can facilitate knowledge transfer and resource sharing, accelerating decarbonization initiatives.
- **Risks/unknowns**: The success of the twinning program depends on the willingness of larger enterprises to engage and the ability of SMEs to leverage the partnerships effectively. Long-term impacts on decarbonization remain to be seen.
- **Tags**: #Decarbonization #SMEs #Collaboration #IRENA
- **Sources**: [IRENA Article](https://www.irena.org/News/articles/2025/Apr/AFID-Enterprise-Twinning-Platform-Connecting-Businesses-for-Industrial-Decarbonization)

## 3. How to Decarbonise Global Transport and Industry
- **Deal Summary**: IRENA outlines strategies for decarbonizing global transport and industry, emphasizing the need for partnerships, policy frameworks, and innovative financing solutions to facilitate the energy transition.
- **Why it matters**: Transport and industry are significant contributors to global greenhouse gas emissions. Effective decarbonization strategies are essential for meeting climate targets and promoting sustainable development.
- **Risks/unknowns**: Implementation of proposed strategies may face challenges such as regulatory hurdles, technological limitations, and varying levels of commitment from different countries. The effectiveness of partnerships in driving change is still uncertain.
- **Tags**: #Decarbonization #Transport #Industry #IRENA
- **Sources**: [IRENA Article](https://www.irena.org/News/articles/2025/May/How-to-decarbonise-global-transport-and-industry)
