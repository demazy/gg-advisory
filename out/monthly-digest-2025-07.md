### NEPKI Initiative for Secure Energy Future
- **SECTION:** Energy Transition
- **PUBLISHED:** 2025-07-10
- **Summary:** The National Energy Public Key Infrastructure (NEPKI) is a new initiative in Australia aimed at securing the clean energy future by enabling secure digital communication between energy networks and consumer devices. This infrastructure supports the integration of consumer energy resources, such as solar panels and electric vehicles, into the electricity grid. NEPKI is part of the National Consumer Energy Resources Roadmap and will be established as a not-for-profit organisation, inviting diverse membership from various energy sectors.
- *Why it matters:*
  - Enhances the security and efficiency of Australia's energy grid.
  - Supports compliance with emerging digital infrastructure regulations.
- *Signals to watch:*
  - Membership applications for NEPKI are now open.
- **Sources:**
  - [Energy Networks Australia](https://www.energynetworks.com.au/news/energy-insider/2025-energy-insider/what-is-nepki-and-how-can-you-help-shape-its-future/)

### Renewable Energy Improves Healthcare in Zimbabwe
- **SECTION:** Sustainable Finance & Investment
- **PUBLISHED:** 2025-07-02
- **Summary:** Decentralised renewable energy solutions have significantly enhanced healthcare services in rural Zimbabwe, supporting over 180,000 deliveries annually. Many health facilities previously struggled with unreliable electricity, impacting critical services. The introduction of renewable energy has improved operational conditions, allowing healthcare workers to provide essential services without interruptions caused by power outages.
- *Why it matters:*
  - Highlights the potential for renewable energy to address healthcare challenges in underserved regions.
  - Encourages investment in renewable solutions that can yield social benefits alongside financial returns.
- *Signals to watch:*
  - Increased interest in renewable energy projects in healthcare sectors across developing regions.
- **Sources:**
  - [IRENA News](https://www.irena.org/News/articles/2021/Jul/How-Decentralised-Renewables-Transform-Healthcare-Services-in-Rural-Zimbabwe)
