- **Headline:** NEPKI Initiative for Australia's Clean Energy Future
  - **SECTION:** Energy Transition
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2025-07-10
  - **Summary:** The National Energy Public Key Infrastructure (NEPKI) is a new initiative aimed at securing Australia's clean energy future by enabling secure digital communication between energy networks and consumer energy resources. NEPKI will facilitate the integration of solar inverters, home batteries, and electric vehicles into the grid. It is backed by the National Consumer Energy Resources Roadmap and aims to enhance the security and interoperability of the energy system.
  - *Why it matters*
    - NEPKI's establishment is crucial for the digital transformation of the energy sector in Australia.
    - It supports compliance with emerging digital security standards in energy markets.
  - **Signals to watch**
    - A final determination from the ACCC regarding NEPKI is expected in July.
  - **Sources**
    - https://www.energynetworks.com.au/news/energy-insider/2025-energy-insider/what-is-nepki-and-how-can-you-help-shape-its-future/

- **Headline:** Decentralised Renewables in Rural Zimbabwe
  - **SECTION:** Energy Transition
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2025-07-02
  - **Summary:** Decentralised renewable energy (DRE) has significantly improved healthcare services in rural Zimbabwe, supporting over 180,000 deliveries annually. Many health facilities faced challenges due to unreliable electricity, impacting care quality. DRE solutions have provided 55% of new electricity connections in Sub-Saharan Africa between 2020 and 2022, demonstrating their potential to enhance healthcare delivery in underserved areas.
  - *Why it matters*
    - This model can inform similar initiatives in AU and EU rural healthcare sectors.
    - Investors may find opportunities in DRE projects that address energy access in developing regions.
  - **Signals to watch**
    - The ongoing expansion of DRE in Sub-Saharan Africa could influence investment trends in renewable energy.
  - **Sources**
    - https://www.irena.org/News/articles/2021/Jul/How-Decentralised-Renewables-Transform-Healthcare-Services-in-Rural-Zimbabwe
