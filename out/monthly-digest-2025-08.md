- **Battery Energy Storage Systems: Key to Renewable Power Supply-Demand Gaps**
  - **SECTION:** Energy Transition
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2025-08-27
  - **Summary:** The integration of renewable energy sources like solar and wind necessitates flexible power grids. Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) are pivotal in addressing supply-demand gaps by storing excess electricity for later use. Since 2018, energy shifting has accounted for 67% of total electricity storage capacity additions in 2024. BESS can be utility-scale or behind-the-meter, serving various applications from grid support to residential energy savings.
  - *Why it matters*
    - Enhances grid reliability, crucial for businesses relying on consistent energy supply.
    - Supports compliance with renewable energy targets in the EU and Australia.
  - **Signals to watch**
    - Monitor developments in BESS technology and capacity expansions.
  - **Sources**

- **Green hydrogen and derived commodities: an opportunity for Global South’s green industrialisation**
  - **SECTION:** Sustainable Finance & Investment
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2025-08-08
  - **Summary:** Green hydrogen, produced using renewable energy, is vital for decarbonising hard-to-abate sectors and fostering green industrialisation in the Global South. It can replace fossil-derived grey hydrogen in industrial processes, contributing to energy independence and job creation. Green hydrogen also offers solutions for seasonal energy storage and is being explored for heavy-duty transport applications.
  - *Why it matters*
    - Positions businesses in the Global South to participate in emerging green markets.
    - Aligns with EU and Australian decarbonisation goals, enhancing investment attractiveness.
  - **Signals to watch**
    - Watch for regulatory frameworks supporting green hydrogen development.
  - **Sources**
    - https://www.irena.org/News/articles/2025/Aug/Green-hydrogen-and-derived-commodities-an-opportunity-for-Global-Souths-green-industralisation

- **Unlocking the Potential of High-Renewable Power Systems with Digital Technologies and Artificial Intelligence**
  - **SECTION:** Energy Transition
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2025-08-01

Digital technologies enhance the integration of variable renewable energy sources into electricity grids, optimizing performance and market competitiveness. AI and smart grid management improve demand forecasting and operational efficiency, ensuring a consistent supply of renewable energy.
