## Monthly Digest - January 2026

### ARENA's Investment in Electric Vehicles
**Source:** [arena.gov.au](https://arena.gov.au/)  
ARENA has announced a significant investment of $21 million aimed at accelerating Australia's transition to electric vehicles (EVs). This funding is part of a broader initiative to enhance the country's renewable energy infrastructure.

- Investment will support the development of EV charging infrastructure.
- Aims to reduce greenhouse gas emissions from the transport sector.
- Enhances the integration of renewable energy sources into the EV ecosystem.

### Grid-Forming Battery Revolution
**Source:** [arena.gov.au](https://arena.gov.au/)  
Australia is positioning itself as a global leader in grid-forming battery technology, transitioning from pilot projects to large-scale implementations. This shift is crucial for stabilizing the energy grid as more renewable sources are integrated.

- Focus on developing technologies that support grid stability.
- Encourages investment in battery storage solutions.
- Aims to enhance energy security and reliability.

### Pilbara Solar Innovation Hub
**Source:** [arena.gov.au](https://arena.gov.au/)  
ARENA has announced a $45 million investment for the Pilbara Solar Innovation Hub, which will serve as a testing ground for new solar technologies and applications.

- The hub will facilitate research and development in solar energy.
- Aims to attract investment and innovation in the renewable sector.
- Expected to contribute to local economic growth and job creation.

### Collaboration in Industrial Decarbonisation
**Source:** [arena.gov.au](https://arena.gov.au/)  
ARENA is emphasizing the importance of collaboration in decarbonizing hard-to-abate industries, such as iron and steel. The agency is fostering partnerships to share risks and accelerate technological advancements.

- Collaboration is essential for overcoming technical and financial barriers.
- Initiatives like NeoSmelt aim to demonstrate viable decarbonization pathways.
- Focus on creating a coordinated approach among industry stakeholders.

### Energy Networks Australia Updates
**Source:** [energynetworks.com.au](https://www.energynetworks.com.au)  
Energy Networks Australia has released its Draft 2026 Integrated System Plan (ISP), which outlines the necessary infrastructure for Australia's energy transition, reducing the projected need for new transmission lines.

- New transmission requirements have been reduced from 10,000 km to 6,000 km.
- Emphasizes the role of distribution networks in supporting renewable integration.
- Aims to keep costs down while ensuring reliable energy supply.

### National Connection Guidelines
**Source:** [energynetworks.com.au](https://www.energynetworks.com.au/guidelines/)  
Energy Networks Australia has launched guidelines for the safe and efficient connection of distributed energy resources (DER) to the grid, marking a significant step towards a more integrated energy system.

- Guidelines provide a consistent approach for connecting solar and battery systems.
- Aims to enhance safety and operational efficiency in energy networks.
- Supports the growth of renewable energy technologies across Australia.

### IFRS Standards for Sustainable Finance
**Source:** [ifrs.org](https://www.ifrs.org)  
The IFRS is promoting the integration of sustainability considerations into financial analysis through its standards, which are crucial for organizations aiming to enhance their ESG reporting.

- Organizations can subscribe to access comprehensive IFRS Standards.
- The FSA Credential is available for professionals focusing on sustainability in finance.
- Emphasizes the importance of transparency in sustainability disclosures.
