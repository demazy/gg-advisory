- **Energy Networks Australia welcomes Solar Sharer Offer next steps**
  - **SECTION:** Energy Transition
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2026-01-23
  - **Summary:** Energy Networks Australia (ENA) has endorsed the Solar Sharer Offer outcomes paper, emphasizing the need to address the disparity in energy access among Australians. ENA CEO Dom van den Berg highlighted that while households with solar installations benefit from reduced energy costs, renters and others without such options face challenges. The initiative aims to create equitable access to clean energy solutions. ENA plans to collaborate with the Australian Energy Regulator to ensure that policy design aligns with market settings and infrastructure investment, thereby maximizing the program's benefits for all Australians.
  - *Why it matters*
    - This initiative could enhance energy affordability for underserved communities in Australia.
    - It signals potential regulatory changes that may affect energy tariffs and infrastructure investments.
  - **Signals to watch**
    - Monitor the alignment of policy and market settings as the program develops.
    - Watch for updates on the six design principles from ENA and the Australian Energy Regulator.
