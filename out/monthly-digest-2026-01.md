## Energy Transition
### Solar Sharer Offer Next Steps Welcomed
- **PUBLISHER:** energynetworks.com.au
- **PUBLISHED:** 2026-01-23
- **Summary:** Energy Networks Australia (ENA) has welcomed the release of the Solar Sharer Offer outcomes paper, emphasizing the importance of addressing energy inequity in Australia’s clean energy transition. ENA CEO Dom van den Berg highlighted the need for policy alignment to ensure the program's success, particularly for households unable to access rooftop solar or electric vehicles. The initiative aims to provide greater network tariff flexibility and support for renters and low-income households.
- **Why it matters:** 
  - Addresses energy access disparities in clean energy transition.
  - Encourages collaboration with regulators for effective policy implementation.
- **Source:** https://www.energynetworks.com.au/news/energy-networks-australia-welcomes-solar-sharer-offer-next-steps/

### Q4 2025 Carbon Market Report Data Workbook Available
- **PUBLISHER:** cer.gov.au

## ESG Reporting
### ISSB Update January 2026
- **PUBLISHER:** ifrs.org
- **PUBLISHED:** 2026-01-01
- **Summary:** The ISSB Update outlines preliminary decisions made by the International Sustainability Standards Board regarding its standard-setting project on nature-related risks and opportunities. The board is moving forward with discussions on developing defined terms for disclosure requirements. The ISSB's decisions are part of its ongoing efforts to enhance sustainability disclosure standards.
- **Why it matters:** 
  - Reflects a commitment to improving sustainability reporting standards.
  - Addresses the growing importance of nature-related risks in financial disclosures.
