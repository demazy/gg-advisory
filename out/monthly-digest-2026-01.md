## Energy Transition
### Q4 2025 Carbon Market Report Available
- **PUBLISHER:** cer.gov.au
- **PUBLISHED:** 2026-01-01
- **Summary:** The Q4 2025 Quarterly Carbon Market Report (QCMR) data workbook is now available, providing early access to information on the Australian carbon market. The full report is expected by mid-March 2026, which will include forecasts and additional commentary. The data workbook will be updated with further data and charts upon the publication of the full report.
- **Why it matters:** 
  - Enhances access to timely information for stakeholders in the carbon market.
  - Supports informed decision-making in carbon trading and compliance.
- **Source:** https://www.cer.gov.au/news-and-media/news/2026/january/explore-q4-2025-qcmr-data-workbook
