## Energy Transition
### Solar Sharer Offer Next Steps Welcomed
- **PUBLISHER:** energynetworks.com.au
- **PUBLISHED:** 2026-01-23
- **Summary:** Energy Networks Australia (ENA) has welcomed the outcomes of the Solar Sharer Offer, emphasizing the need to address the energy inequity between households that can afford solar solutions and those that cannot. ENA CEO Dom van den Berg stated that policy design must align with market settings to ensure the program's success. The initiative aims to provide greater network tariff flexibility and support for renters and others unable to install solar systems.
- **Why it matters:** 
  - Highlights the gap in access to clean energy solutions.
  - Aims to create equitable energy policies for all Australians.
- **Source:** https://www.energynetworks.com.au/news/energy-networks-australia-welcomes-solar-sharer-offer-next-steps/
