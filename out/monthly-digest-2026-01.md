- **Energy Networks Australia welcomes Solar Sharer Offer**
  - **SECTION:** Energy Transition
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2026-01-23
  - **Summary:** Energy Networks Australia (ENA) has expressed support for the Solar Sharer Offer outcomes paper, emphasizing the need to bridge the energy access gap in Australia. ENA CEO Dom van den Berg highlighted that while some households benefit from solar and electric vehicles, others, particularly renters, lack these options. The initiative aims to create equitable energy access and requires alignment of policy, infrastructure investment, and energy tariffs for success. ENA is committed to collaborating with the Australian Energy Regulator to ensure effective implementation.
  - *Why it matters*
    - This initiative could reshape energy access for Australian renters and low-income households.
    - Aligning energy tariffs with policy could enhance investment opportunities in renewable energy sectors.
  - **Signals to watch**
    - Monitor the development of market settings and policy designs to support the Solar Sharer Offer.
  - **Sources**
    - https://www.energynetworks.com.au/news/energy-networks-australia-welcomes-solar-sharer-offer-next-steps/
