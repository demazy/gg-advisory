## Monthly Digest - January 2026

### ARENA's Investment in Electric Vehicles
**Source:** [arena.gov.au](https://arena.gov.au/)  
ARENA has announced a significant investment of $21 million aimed at accelerating Australia's transition to electric vehicles (EVs). This funding is part of a broader strategy to enhance the renewable energy ecosystem in Australia.

- Investment will support the development of EV infrastructure.
- Aims to promote the adoption of electric vehicles among Australian consumers.
- Part of a larger initiative to transition to renewable energy sources.

### Accessibility Commitment by ARENA
**Source:** [arena.gov.au](https://arena.gov.au/accessibility/)  
ARENA is dedicated to ensuring that its website is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. The site adheres to the Digital Service Standard and Web Content Accessibility Guidelines.

- Focus on minimizing download times and ensuring compatibility with popular web browsers.
- Provides alternative formats for downloadable files when possible.
- Offers keyboard navigation and screen reader support for improved accessibility.

### Collaboration for Industrial Decarbonisation
**Source:** [arena.gov.au](https://arena.gov.au/)  
ARENA is emphasizing the importance of collaboration in decarbonizing hard-to-abate industries such as iron, steel, and alumina. This approach aims to share risks and pool expertise across sectors.

- Initiatives like NeoSmelt and the Australian Alumina Working Group are examples of structured collaboration.
- Collaboration is essential for overcoming technical complexities and high capital requirements.
- Focus on accelerating the deployment of emerging technologies in heavy industries.

### Draft Integrated System Plan (ISP) Released
**Source:** [energynetworks.com.au](https://www.energynetworks.com.au/news/draft-isp-aims-to-right-size-transmission-and-reinforces-networks-as-the-backbone-of-the-energy-transition/)  
The Draft 2026 Integrated System Plan (ISP) has been released, indicating a reduced need for new transmission infrastructure from 10,000 km to 6,000 km. This plan aims to optimize the energy transition in Australia.

- Highlights the critical role of transmission networks in integrating renewable energy.
- Emphasizes the importance of distribution networks in reducing transmission investment needs.
- Aims to keep costs down while achieving net-zero emissions.

### Energy Networks Australia Guidelines
**Source:** [energynetworks.com.au](https://www.energynetworks.com.au/guidelines/)  
Energy Networks Australia has published guidelines for the energy industry, focusing on the safe and efficient connection of distributed energy resources (DER) to the grid.

- Guidelines cover various topics, including safety and technical specifications.
- Aimed at ensuring a consistent approach to DER installations across Australia.
- Available through subscription or purchase via Intertek Inform.

### Renewable Energy Content Feed
**Source:** [arena.gov.au](https://arena.gov.au/blog)  
ARENA provides a comprehensive feed of renewable energy content, including blogs, podcasts, and videos, aimed at educating and informing the public about renewable energy developments.

- Content covers a wide range of topics, including battery storage, electric vehicles, and solar energy.
- Offers insights into ongoing projects and innovations in the renewable energy sector.
- Encourages public engagement and awareness of renewable energy initiatives.

### CEFC Innovation Fund
**Source:** [arena.gov.au](https://www.cefc.com.au/where-we-invest/sustainable-economy/innovation-fund/)  
The Clean Energy Finance Corporation (CEFC) has established an Innovation Fund to support sustainable economic growth through investment in innovative technologies.

- Fund aims to drive advancements in clean energy technologies.
- Focus on projects that contribute to Australia's transition to a sustainable economy.
- Encourages collaboration between industry and research institutions.
