## EFRAG Update Podcast Highlights

**Headline:** EFRAG Podcast Discusses Reporting Standards

The latest EFRAG Update podcast features discussions on the EU Voluntary Reporting Template for SMEs and Start-Ups, alongside a public consultation on amendments to the Reduced Disclosure Standard. The episode also highlights the upcoming EFRAG Conference on December 4, which will celebrate the simplified draft European Sustainability Reporting Standards (ESRS). Educational materials on the VSME market study were also released, aiming to enhance understanding of sustainability reporting.

*Why it matters:*
- Businesses must adapt to evolving reporting standards to ensure compliance.
- Engaging in consultations can influence the development of reporting frameworks.

*Signals to watch:*
- EFRAG Conference on December 4, 2025.
- Public consultation deadlines for the Reduced Disclosure Standard.

## IFASS Meetings Overview

**Headline:** IFASS Meetings Address Global Reporting Issues

The International Forum of Accounting Standard Setters (IFASS) provides a platform for national accounting standard setters to discuss developments in corporate reporting. EFRAG's Chiara Del Prete serves as the IFASS Chair until March 2025. Recent meetings have focused on enhancing collaboration between national and international standard-setting bodies, including the IASB and ISSB.

*Why it matters:*
- Participation in IFASS meetings can shape national and international reporting standards.
- Businesses should stay informed about global trends to align their reporting practices.

*Signals to watch:*
- Next IFASS meeting scheduled for March 2025 in Naples, Italy.
- Reports from past meetings available for review.
