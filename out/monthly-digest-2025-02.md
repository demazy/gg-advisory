- **Tripling off-grid renewables is a catalyst for sustainable development in rural communities**
  - **SECTION:** Energy Transition
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2025-02-24
  - **Summary:** The COP28 UAE consensus aims to triple installed renewable energy capacity to 11.2 terawatts and double energy efficiency improvements by 2030. Off-grid renewables are vital for enhancing electricity access, especially in rural Sub-Saharan Africa, which now accounts for 83% of the global access deficit. Off-grid solutions like solar home systems and mini-grids have already benefited 155 million people in 2023, driving progress in multiple Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), including poverty alleviation and gender equity.
  - *Why it matters*
    - Australian and European businesses can leverage off-grid solutions to meet sustainability targets.
    - Investors may find opportunities in emerging markets focused on renewable energy projects.
  - **Signals to watch**
    - Monitor progress towards the 2030 renewable energy capacity goals.
    - Watch for developments in off-grid renewable projects in Sub-Saharan Africa.
