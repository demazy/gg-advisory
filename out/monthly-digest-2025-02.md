### Top Items

- **Tripling off-grid renewables is a catalyst for sustainable development in rural communities**
  - **SECTION:** Sustainable Finance & Investment
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2025-02-24
  - **Summary:** The COP28 UAE consensus aims to triple global renewable energy capacity to 11.2 terawatts and double energy efficiency improvements by 2030. Off-grid renewable solutions, such as solar home systems and mini-grids, are essential for expanding electricity access, particularly in rural Sub-Saharan Africa, which now accounts for 83% of the global access deficit. These systems have already benefited 155 million people in 2023, driving progress in multiple Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), including poverty alleviation, gender equity, and food security.
  - *Why it matters*  
    - Australian and European businesses can leverage off-grid solutions to meet sustainability targets and enhance community engagement.  
    - Investors may find opportunities in financing off-grid projects that align with SDG objectives.
  - *Signals to watch*  
    - Monitor the implementation timelines for the COP28 commitments and related funding opportunities.  
    - Assess the potential for partnerships in off-grid renewable projects in emerging markets.
