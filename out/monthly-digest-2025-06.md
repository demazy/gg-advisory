### IRENA Calls for Renewable Energy Projects
- **SECTION:** Sustainable Finance & Investment
- **PUBLISHED:** 2025-06-24
- **Summary:** IRENA has launched a call for project proposals to accelerate renewable energy deployment in Africa ahead of the second APRA Investment Forum in Sierra Leone, scheduled for October 20-23, 2025. Projects must align with sustainable development goals and be beyond the conceptual stage. Eligible categories include renewable energy generation, grid infrastructure, e-mobility, and critical minerals. Selected projects will receive support in documentation and funding meetings.
- *Why it matters:*
  - Australian and European investors may find new opportunities in African renewable projects.
  - Aligning with global sustainability goals can enhance corporate reputations and compliance.
- **Signals to watch:**
  - Project submission deadline is August 31, 2025.
- **Sources:**
  - [IRENA News](https://www.irena.org/News/articles/2025/Jun/IRENA-Launches-Call-for-Projects-Ahead-of-APRA-Investment-Forum-in-Sierra-Leone)

### Key Takeaways from IRENA Innovation Week 2025
- **SECTION:** Sustainable Finance & Investment
- **PUBLISHED:** 2025-06-16
- **Summary:** IRENA Innovation Week 2025 focused on the urgent need for innovation in renewable energy deployment, particularly in the Global South. Key discussions included the importance of aligning renewable energy with social equity and local value creation. The event highlighted the goal of tripling renewable power capacity by 2030 to limit global temperature rise to 1.5°C, emphasizing a holistic approach to innovation that encompasses policy and market design.
- *Why it matters:*
  - Australian and European businesses must adapt to evolving energy policies and market demands.
  - Investors should consider the implications of social equity in renewable projects for long-term viability.
- **Signals to watch:**
  - The 2030 target for tripling renewable capacity is a critical deadline for stakeholders.
- **Sources:**
  - [IRENA News](https://www.irena.org/News/articles/2025/Jun/Innovation-Week-2025-Wrap-Up)

### Innovation in Renewable Energy Trade
- **SECTION:** Sustainable Finance & Investment
- **PUBLISHED:** 2025-06-13
- **Summary:** Day 3 of IRENA Innovation Week focused on how the energy transition is reshaping global trade dynamics, particularly in renewable energy and hydrogen markets. Emerging trade patterns are expected as countries with abundant renewable resources become exporters, while resource-limited nations will increasingly rely on imports. The session emphasized the need for sustainable and resilient value chains in this evolving landscape.
- *Why it matters:*
  - Australian and European businesses must adapt to new trade dynamics and consider international partnerships.
  - Understanding these shifts can help investors identify emerging markets and opportunities.
- **Signals to watch:**
  - Ongoing developments in renewable energy trade agreements may impact market access and pricing.
- **Sources:**
  - [IRENA News](https://www.irena.org/News/articles/2025/Jun/Innovation-Week-2025-As-it-Happens-Day-3)
