- **IRENA Launches Call for Projects Ahead of the Second APRA Investment Forum**
  - **SECTION:** Energy Transition
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2025-06-24
  - **Summary:** IRENA invites project developers to submit proposals for renewable energy initiatives ahead of the APRA Investment Forum in Sierra Leone, scheduled for October 20-23, 2025. Submissions are due by August 31, 2025, under the Climate Investment Platform or the Energy Transition Accelerator Financing Platform. The initiative aims to support Africa's clean energy transition, building on the inaugural forum held in Nairobi in 2024.
  - *Why it matters:*
    - This initiative could attract investment into African renewable projects, presenting opportunities for Australian and European investors.
    - Compliance with sustainable development goals is essential for project eligibility, influencing investment strategies.
  - **Signals to watch:**
    - Submission deadline: August 31, 2025.
