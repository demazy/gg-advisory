- **IRENA Launches Call for Projects Ahead of the Second APRA Investment Forum in Sierra Leone**
  - **SECTION:** Energy Transition
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2025-06-24
  - **Summary:** IRENA invites project developers to submit proposals for renewable energy initiatives ahead of the APRA Investment Forum in Sierra Leone, scheduled for October 20-23, 2025. Submissions are due by August 31, 2025, under the Climate Investment Platform (CIP) or Energy Transition Accelerator Financing (ETAF) Platform. The forum aims to support Africa's clean energy transition, building on the inaugural forum in Nairobi, Kenya, in 2024.
  - *Why it matters:*
    - This initiative could enhance investment opportunities for Australian and European businesses in Africa's renewable sector.
    - Compliance with sustainable development goals is crucial for project eligibility, impacting investors' strategies.
  - **Signals to watch:**
    - Submission deadline: August 31, 2025.
    - Forum dates: October 20-23, 2025.

- **Five Key Takeaways from the IRENA Innovation Week 2025**
  - **SECTION:** Energy Transition
  - **PUBLISHED:** 2025-06-16
  - **Summary:** The IRENA Innovation Week 2025 highlighted the need for aligning renewable energy deployment with social equity and local value creation. Key discussions focused on systemic innovation, emphasizing that technology alone is insufficient for the energy transition. The event underscored the importance of digital technologies and AI in managing renewable energy, while also addressing the challenges posed by rising energy demands from data centers.
  - *Why it matters:*
    - Australian and European businesses must adapt to the evolving energy landscape shaped by innovation and digitalization.
    - Policymakers need to consider inclusive approaches to ensure equitable energy transitions.
  - **Signals to watch:**
    - Ongoing discussions on integrating digital technologies with energy infrastructure.
  - **Sources:**
    - https://www.irena.org/News/articles/2025/Jun/Innovation-Week-2025-Wrap-Up
